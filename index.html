I am currently an undergraduate student in the College of Computer Science at Qinghai University, supervised by Dr. Zhengjun Du and Dr. Song Wang. I also collaborate closely with Dr. Man Yao and Dr. Hu Tianxiang from the Institute of Automation at the Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research interests primarily include image processing, multimodal learning, and Brain-inspired deep learning.
